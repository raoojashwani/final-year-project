Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book Thread & Knot?

For destination weddings, we recommend reaching out 8 to 12 months in advance to secure premium heritage venues and top-tier celebrity artists. For city weddings, 6 months is standard.

Do you manage international destination weddings?

Yes. While our stronghold is India (Rajasthan, Goa, Kerala), we frequently engineer luxury weddings in Dubai, Thailand, and select European destinations using our global vendor network.

Can you handle large-scale guest logistics (flights & hotels)?

Absolutely. Our dedicated hospitality desk manages end-to-end guest RSVPs, airport transfers, hotel check-ins, and bespoke welcome hampers, ensuring your guests experience seamless luxury.

How does the design and decor process work?

We don't use templates. Our in-house design architects build 3D stage renders and detailed mood boards based on your cultural background and personal aesthetic before construction begins.

How do you handle vendor payments and budgeting?

We pride ourselves on transparent, open-book budgeting. You pay vendors directly based on our negotiated, vetted rates, ensuring you get the absolute best value with zero hidden markups.

Is there an on-site command team during the wedding week?

Yes. A dedicated 'Shadow Team' is deployed on the ground. We handle the timelines, artist cues, and caterer synchronization so the bride, groom, and families can simply be guests.
