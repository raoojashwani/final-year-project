Thread & Knot

The Island Buyout

Private Wedding • 300 Guests • 4 Days

The Challenge

Transporting and housing 300 VIP guests to a remote private island, while importing 5 tons of fresh floral decor and constructing a floating glass mandap over the ocean.

The Solution

Deployed a dedicated logistics fleet of 15 speedboats. Our engineering team constructed a reinforced sub-surface platform for the mandap, passing rigorous safety compliance.

The Multi-City Broadcast

Tech Corporation • 5,000+ Delegates • Hybrid

The Challenge

Executing a synchronized product launch happening simultaneously in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, requiring zero-latency hybrid streaming and identical stage setups.

The Solution

Established a Central Command Center in Gurugram monitoring 3 distinct production crews. Utilized enterprise-grade encrypted satellite streaming to link the venues perfectly.
