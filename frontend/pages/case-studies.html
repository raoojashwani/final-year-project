Deep Dive I

The Jaipur Royal Wedding

Thread & Knot Destination

Project Scope

  • Client: Private Family
  • Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan
  • Scale: 450+ VIP Guests
  • Duration: 3 Days, 4 Venues

The Verdict

"C&O Group didn't just plan a wedding; they built a flawless, immersive world for our family to exist in for three days."

1. The Objective

Execute a flawless, multi-generational royal wedding inside an active 18th-century heritage palace, requiring absolute discretion and unprecedented structural decor without altering the historic site.

2. The Logistical Friction

  • Managing seamless airport transfers for 450+ international guests arriving simultaneously.
  • The venue had zero load-in access for heavy trucks; 15 tons of floral decor had to be transported via specialized hand-carts.
  • Coordinating strict technical riders for a Tier-1 celebrity artist performing at the Sangeet.

3. The Architectural Solution

We deployed a 40-person "Shadow Team" utilizing digital fleet-tracking to map arrivals. For the spatial design, our architects created 3D CAD renders to pre-approve the weight of a 40-foot floating floral mandap on the palace lake, built entirely off-site and assembled modularly.

Deep Dive II

Global Tech Synergy Summit

1. The Objective

Design and execute a high-impact product launch and leadership summit for a Fortune 500 tech firm, requiring simultaneous live-streaming to 5,000 remote employees globally.

2. The Logistical Friction

  • The client required a massive 60-foot curved LED stage for immersive presentations.
  • The venue's acoustic properties caused severe echo, threatening the clarity of the global live-stream broadcast feed.
  • Strict media embargoes required absolute security clearance for all 1,200 physical delegates.

3. The Architectural Solution

Prism Planner deployed a proprietary acoustic baffling system hidden within the brand fabrication. We integrated a dual-channel broadcast matrix, ensuring the physical delegates felt the bass of the launch, while remote viewers received a crystal-clear, studio-grade audio feed. Digital QR-badge scanning secured the perimeter.
