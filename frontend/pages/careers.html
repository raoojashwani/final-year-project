Life at C&O

The Hustle Behind
The Glamour.

We are a collective of designers, planners, engineers, and strategists.

Working at C&O Group is not for the faint of heart. It is fast-paced, high-stakes, and deeply rewarding. Whether you are drafting a 3D mandap for a royal wedding or calling cues for a global tech summit, your work directly impacts milestone moments in people's lives.

We thrive on innovation, obsessive collaboration, and an absolute refusal to settle for "good enough."
